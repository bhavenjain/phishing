WHAT THIS PROCESS IS ALL ABOUT



Our prototype website allows you to paste text in a box and it will read and sift through the text to find the relevant information to tell us whether it is a phishing/scam email or not. Furthermore, if it is a phishing email it tells you the website or company it is trying to impersonate, our website also shows the company that has been impersonated the most. Our app is more useful in our daily live since most of us work on our phones more than our laptops. It identifies texts from unknown sources and automatically identifies if it is a phishing attempt or not. For known contacts, the option to verify messages and various links is given to the user.
Our prototype identifies phishing and scam emails, links, URLs on your website and mobile phones. For anyone that is connected to the internet is liable to receive phishing messages and our solution helps us prevent new ways of phishing attacks to succeed while keeping the old ones from resurging.
